Types of indexes in mongoDB:

1. Single Field Indexes
    a. Product sku
    b. Order date
    c. Order status
2. Compound Indexes (ESR rule)
    a. Product main and sub category
    b. Order customer email and order date
3. Multikey Indexes
    a. User addresses zipcodes
    b. Order items sku
4. Text Indexes
    a. Product name and description
5. Geospatial Indexes
6. Hashed Indexes
7. Unique Indexes
    a. Product sku
    b. User email
8. Partial Indexes
9. Sparse Indexes
10. TTL Indexes
11. Hidden Indexes
12. Case-Insensitive Indexes
    a. User email
